The Kansas City Chiefs

The Chiefs are an American Football team in the National Football League. They are based in Kansas City, Missouri.

Team History

The Chiefs started life in 1960 as the Dallas Texans, a founding member of the upstart American Football League. In 1963 the team relocated to Kansas City and changed their name to the Chiefs. They joined the NFL during the league merger of 1970.
